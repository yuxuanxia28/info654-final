the 2010's

about

the 2010s was characterized by the aftermath of the Great Recession of 2008. According to wikipedia, this decade saw the musical and cultural dominance of dance-pop, electronic dance music, hipster culture and electropop. This era was also defined by gifs and memes. Try searching a gif at the top left corner of the page :)

  • 2010: instagram is founded
  • 2011: beyonce announces her pregnancy at the vma's
  • 2012: carly rae jepsen releases 'call me maybe'

  • 2016: beyonce drops 'lemonade'
  • 2017: serena williams wins the australian open while pregnant
  • 2018: 'queer eye' reboot
